My Story

I was born in Caracas, Venezuela. I came to the United States not too long ago to study computer science. However, unlike most people studying computer science, college was my introduction to programming in general. I have always had a knack for logic, math, and science, as well as being an avid gamer and technology enthusiast, Despite not having any experience in it, I did know I would enjoy programming.

I never thought to learn to program on my own, because I have always been systematic about learning and did not want to learn from random tutorials. I am glad I waited because now I feel like I have a solid foundation in programming. Now I am not only in love with programming, but I discovered a passion for networking, web development, and languages.
